Thinking Arby's? We are too!

Taking someone to a place they haven't been to has been a LinkedIn UED tradition since it's infancy. While now we employe the Wheel of Lunch'In to determine the welcome lunch, there was an era where every Monday was a trip to the local Arby's. It holds a special place in our heart, and we want to share that special place with you.

Our local Arby's is the one on South Bernardo, where you can eat your curly fries beneath the atrium or sip a Jamocha shake in the comfortable booths. Beef and cheddar never tasted so good!

Angus Beef is regarded as some of the best beef in the world for its fine quality, amazing flavor, and exquisite marbling. At Arby's, we only serve 100% Black Angus beef, lightly seasoned with just the right touch of our special blend of cracked black pepper, herbs and spices. Our premium 94% lean top round is oven roasted to perfection and served freshly sliced for the "ultimate" in Angus.

Wanna feel great? Grab an Arby's® Roast Beef Sandwich. It's slow roasted for three hours in our stores every day, freshly sliced and piled high to roast beef perfection. If one of these doesn't put you in a good mood, nothing will.

These are the feel-good sandwiches of the year. When thick cut honey wheat bread meets generous cuts of oven roasted meats and fresh produce, the result is one serious sandwich.

All white meat, chicken breast fillets are the epitome of juicy deliciousness. These sandwiches are sure to put your taste buds in a good mood.

Value Menu
